History: Wigs have been around for centuries, originating in ancient Egypt and Rome. They were a symbol of social status until the end of the 18th century. Purpose Wigs are worn for a variety of reasons, including: Style: To create a style that might not be possible otherwise Hair loss: To avoid damaging natural hair or to restore a sense of normalcy for people experiencing hair loss due to medical conditions or treatments Costume: As part of a costume or disguise Types There are several types of wigs, including wefted, monofilament, double monofilament, and fully hand tied. Wefted wigs are the cheapest to manufacture and buy. Quality A good quality wig should look and feel like your own hair, and be manageable enough to wash and style. A wig that feels dry and hard when you run your fingers through it is probably low quality. Wearing tips If you plan on wearing a wig daily, you should make sure it fits correctly. You can also wear a wig cap liner, wash it when necessary, and store it safely.
